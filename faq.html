Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental includes delivery and pickup, setup and takedown, high-quality paper and soap products, and clean, odor-controlled restrooms.

Do you provide power and water at the event?

Power and water services are available as additional options. We can provide generators and water tanks if the event site lacks these utilities.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance to ensure availability, especially for peak event seasons.

Is there an attendant available?

Yes, we offer concierge services with a full-time attendant to maintain cleanliness and assist guests during your event.

Can I request custom services?

Absolutely! We offer additional services such as background music, fresh flowers, and premium amenities through our Pampered Guest Package.
